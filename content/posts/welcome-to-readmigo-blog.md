Welcome to the official Readmigo blog!

This is where we'll share product updates, reading insights, and our perspectives on how AI is transforming English learning worldwide.

## What to Expect

- **Product Updates** — New features, platform improvements, and roadmap highlights
- **Reading Insights** — Tips, strategies, and inspiration for English learners at every level
- **Market Perspectives** — Trends in EdTech, AI-powered learning, and the future of digital reading

## About Readmigo

Readmigo is an AI-native English reading app with 100,000+ classic books from Project Gutenberg and 20,000+ audiobooks from LibriVox. Available on iOS, Web, and Android (coming soon).
