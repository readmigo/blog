We believe the greatest works of human civilization should be accessible to everyone — not locked behind a paywall.

Starting today, **every book in Readmigo is free**. No limits. No walls. No catch.

## What's Changing

We've completely reimagined how Readmigo works. Here's the short version:

**Free for everyone, forever:**
- **1,200+ classic English books** — read them all, no restrictions
- **Automatic offline caching** — chapters are cached as you read, so you can always pick up where you left off, even without internet
- **30 minutes of free listening every day** — audiobooks and text-to-speech, combined

**Pro subscribers get:**
- **Unlimited listening** — no daily time cap on audiobooks or TTS
- **Cross-device data sync** — your highlights, reading progress, notes, and bookshelf travel with you across every device
- **Cloud backup** — never lose a single highlight, even if you lose your phone
- **One-click download** — download full books with a single tap for complete offline access
- **AES-256 data protection** — military-grade encryption for your reading data

## Why We Made This Change

When we started Readmigo, we gated books behind a subscription. It felt like the standard playbook. But it also felt wrong.

These are public domain classics — Shakespeare, Austen, Dickens, Twain. Works that have shaped human thought for centuries. They don't belong behind a lock.

So we opened the gates. Every book. Every reader. Every device.

**The achievements of human civilization should benefit everyone.** We want to take on that responsibility — making great literature accessible to all, right from the palm of your hand.

## 30 Minutes of Free Listening, Every Day

Reading isn't the only way to experience a book. With Readmigo, you can also listen.

Free users now get **30 minutes per day** of audiobook playback and AI-powered text-to-speech — combined. That's about 15 hours a month. Enough to finish a short novel, one commute at a time.

Pro subscribers, of course, get unlimited listening with no daily cap.

## Your Data, Your Choice

Here's where Pro really shines. As you read, you naturally create valuable data — highlights, notes, reading progress, bookshelf organization. All of this is stored locally on your device.

But what happens when you get a new phone? Or want to continue reading on your iPad? Or — worst case — your device breaks?

With a Pro subscription, all your reading data syncs across every device, backed up safely in the cloud. **Subscribe once, and every highlight you've ever made appears instantly.** We silently save your data from day one, so nothing is ever lost.

## Lowering the Barrier to Learning

Our mission is simple: **lower the barrier to learning English through reading.**

A tap on your screen. A page in a classic novel. An AI that explains any word or sentence. A voice that reads to you when your eyes are tired.

That's all it takes. And now, it's free.

## Get Started

Readmigo is available on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/readmigo/id6740539519) and [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.readmigo.app). Update to the latest version and start reading — everything is already unlocked.

Happy reading.
