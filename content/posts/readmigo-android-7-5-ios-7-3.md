This is the update we've been building toward. Starting today, **every book in Readmigo is completely free** — no subscription, no limits, no exceptions. Android 7.5 and iOS 7.3 roll out a redesigned freemium model that puts great literature first and saves Pro for the features that truly matter.

## What's New for Everyone

The core reading experience is now fully open.

**Every e-book is free.** All 1,200+ classics in the Readmigo library — from Shakespeare to Twain, Austen to Tolstoy — are yours to read without restriction. No trial periods. No chapter locks. Just open a book and start reading.

**30 minutes of free listening every day.** Audiobooks and text-to-speech share a combined daily allowance. That's roughly 15 hours a month — enough to work through a novel on your daily commute or wind down before bed.

**Chapter caching stays free.** As you read, chapters are automatically cached on your device so you can pick up where you left off — even without internet. No Pro subscription needed.

## Redesigned Pro

We've completely rethought what Pro means. Instead of gating books, **Pro is now about protecting and syncing your data** across every device you own.

Here's what Pro subscribers get:

- **Cross-device data sync** — Your highlights, notes, reading progress, and bookshelf organization travel with you. Start on your phone, continue on your tablet, and never miss a beat.
- **Cloud backup** — Every highlight, every note, every bookmark — safely stored in the cloud. Switch phones, lose a device, or just want peace of mind? Your reading history is always recoverable.
- **Unlimited audio** — No daily cap on audiobooks or TTS. Listen as much as you want, whenever you want.
- **One-click download** — Download an entire book with a single tap for full offline access. (Free users still get automatic chapter caching as they read.)
- **AES-256 data encryption** — Your synced reading data is protected with military-grade encryption.

We silently save your reading data from day one, even on a free account. **The moment you subscribe, everything syncs instantly** — nothing is ever lost.

## Highlight Style Picker (iOS)

iOS users get a new highlight style picker with **four distinct styles** to choose from. Whether you prefer a classic yellow highlight, a subtle underline, or a bold color accent, you can now personalize how your annotations look across every book.

This brings highlight styling on iOS in line with the Android app, so your reading experience feels consistent no matter which device you're using.

## Under the Hood

A few important improvements that make the app more reliable:

- **More accurate audio tracking** — We rebuilt how listening time is measured to ensure your 30-minute daily allowance is tracked reliably, even if the app is backgrounded or your device restarts. The timer just works.
- **Better bookmark storage (iOS)** — Bookmarks have been migrated to a faster, more robust database. You won't notice anything different — except that everything is a little snappier.
- **Smarter push notifications (iOS)** — The app now sends your device language when registering for notifications, so you'll receive messages in the right language from day one.
- **Stability fixes (Android)** — Resolved edge cases with network responses and data handling that could occasionally cause errors.
